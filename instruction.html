Instructions for Online Examination

Please read the instructions carefully before starting the examination.

  1. Click on Start Exam button at the bottom of your screen to begin the examination.
  2. The clock has been set at the server, and the countdown timer at the top right of the screen will display the remaining time.
  3. Click on Next button to move to the next question.
  4. The color-coded diagram on the right side of the screen shows the status of questions:
    		Answered / Attempted Questions.
    		Revisit Questions.
    		Not visited.
  5. All answered questions will be counted for calculating the final score.
  6. Do not click End Exam before completing the test. Once ended, you will not be allowed to rejoin.
  7. Ensure you're alone and free from distractions.
  8. Do not refresh or close the window once the exam starts.
  9. Switching tabs may result in automatic termination.
  10. Avoid using mobile phones or external devices.
  11. Ensure your internet connection is stable throughout the test.
  12. Once the exam starts, the timer will not pause.
